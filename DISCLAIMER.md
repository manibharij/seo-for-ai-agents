# Disclaimer

**Read this before relying on anything this pack produces.**

## Experimental and early-stage

`seo-for-ai-agents` is **experimental** and under active development. It is offered in good faith as a set of guidance skills for AI coding agents — not as a finished or guaranteed product. Treat its output as a strong, well-informed **starting point that you review**, not as a final authority to apply blindly. Use caution, especially on production sites.

## AI agents make mistakes — human evaluation is required

These skills are executed by **AI coding agents** (Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, Copilot and others). AI agents can misread a codebase, apply a change incorrectly, misjudge context, or appear to "succeed" while breaking something in practice. **A human must evaluate what an agent does before trusting or publishing it.** In particular:

- **Verify on the served output**, exactly as the skills instruct — confirm the actual rendered/served result, never just that the source was edited.
- **Review every change** — read the diff and test the site — before you deploy.
- **Keep your work in version control** and have backups, so every change is reversible.
- Take special care wherever a skill **flags a human decision** (architecture changes, anything touching trust/E-E-A-T, robots/indexing rules, AI-crawler access). Those are deliberately not for the agent to settle alone.

This pack is built to *reduce* the chance of an agent doing the wrong thing — by enforcing an order of operations and by verifying on what's actually served — but it cannot remove the need for a human in the loop.

## Designed with a professional SEO mindset — but still guidance

The method (the [Visibility Ladder](METHOD.md)), its dependency order, and its priorities reflect the judgment of an experienced SEO practitioner — roughly fifteen years of hands-on practice — and are deliberately built to avoid the lazy flat-checklist approach most AI-generated SEO advice falls into. That design intent raises the quality of the guidance.

It does not, however, change three things: the guidance is **general**, your situation is **specific**, and the execution is by an **AI agent**. Experienced judgment informs the map; you and your context still have to drive. Where the recommendations and your own knowledge of your site disagree, use your judgment.

## No guarantee of results

SEO and AEO outcomes depend on many factors outside this pack's control — the evolving systems of search engines and AI answer engines, your competitors, your market, and your off-site authority. **Nothing here guarantees rankings, traffic, rich results, or citations by AI answer engines.** The skills fix build-time, owned-media problems and improve *eligibility*; whether that translates into live results is never promised, and can only be established with live measurement (which is outside the scope of these free, build-time skills).
